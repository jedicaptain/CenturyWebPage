About Us

Our Story

Century Excavation Inc. began when, as professional pipe layers and foremen working on major excavation and plumbing projects, our team quickly recognized the good and the bad of the industry.

They set out to make a difference by delivering better, higher quality work than anyone else. They learned to complete projects that rarely required fixes or changes and focused on creating strong relationships through excellent service.

Today, Century Excavation Inc. leads Utah in excavation services. With employees and customers who praise the quality of work done by Century Excavation, you can expect excellence during every stage of your next project. Check out our sister company UMC, Inc. for HVAC and Plumbing needs.

Our Purpose

When starting your venture, you have a vision for how the job will be done and how you want to be treated. Understanding this, we aim to deliver timely and quality service with every project. Through building a reputation of honesty and commitment to our customers, we have been able to expand our operations and relationships.
